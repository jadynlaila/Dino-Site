Brachiosaurus

  • This lovable creature grows to be around 65 feet. When they are full grown they can range in weight anywhere from 62,000 tons to 128,000 tons. They are built much like giraffes, they have huge necks and smaller tails. They like to spend a lot of their time in water but also can easily adapt to heat. They are herbivores and require about 900 pounds of dry plants every day. They are said to be one of the sweetest dinosaurs in the bunch and they love playing with kids. They are extremely gentle and become protective of their owners after a little while of being with them.
