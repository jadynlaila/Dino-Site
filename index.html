Dino Care

Taking proper care of your new dino from the very start is absolutely critical to its health and well-being. We strongly advise you to do heavy research before making the decision to purchase a dino. Not only will your dinosaur's health be at risk, but yours will be as well if you don't feed them properly! They will find a food source by all means...
Basic care requirements state that you must have over 5 acres of land and access to 25 gallons of both food and water daily! Care requirements vary greatly between different dinos, so we advise you to check out your local library or credible websites and get to studying! It's for your own good!
